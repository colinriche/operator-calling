\## Product Concept



The Operator is a \*\*voice-first communication platform\*\* inspired by the early spirit of Skype before it became bloated.



It is designed around:

\- Proper phone calls instead of endless typed messaging

\- Scheduling calls at times that suit both people

\- Callbacks without awkward timing

\- Privacy-focused unexpected one-to-one calls with unknown people globally

\- Selective one-to-one calls within admin-controlled groups



\### Key Mechanic

The Operator \*\*only connects a call when both users answer\*\*, removing pressure and missed timing issues.
